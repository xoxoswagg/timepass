— Our Story

Rooted in
Passion

Maison Noir was born from a simple obsession: the perfect cup of coffee. Founded in the heart of the city, we source our beans from small, sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

Every element of your experience — from the precise extraction of each espresso to the buttery layers of our croissants — is executed with uncompromising care and expertise.
